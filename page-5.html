Here are the steps:

Use ProPublica’s ER Inspector to research the emergency room that works best for you and your family. We explain how to choose below.

Once you've picked, print out the worksheet and write down the information for your preferred hospital, your doctor, and your emergency contacts.

Post the worksheet on your refrigerator or near your first aid kit.

Share this page with others in your community, or fill out the worksheet for your family members. Make sure to update the information every six months!
